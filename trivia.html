In Spain, people have a given name (nombre) and two family names or surnames (apellidos), each one of these corresponding to the first surname of each parent.
My full name is, then: Miguel Muñoz Pérez.

My preferred way of signing is 'M. Muñoz Pérez', even if sometimes I may appear as 'M. M. Pérez' or as 'M. Muñoz-Pérez' in some places. I would cite my own papers by refering to the first surname, i.e. as [MuXX].
